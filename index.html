Exercise is essential for people working on computers all day, as it combats the effects of prolonged sitting. It improves circulation, strengthens muscles, and reduces common issues like back pain, neck strain, and stiffness from sitting too long.

Physical activity also boosts mental well-being by reducing stress and enhancing mood through the release of endorphins. It helps prevent burnout and improves focus, creativity, and productivity.

Regular exercise increases energy levels, fighting off fatigue and sluggishness common after long hours at a screen. Even short sessions can boost alertness and overall well-being for computer-based workers.
